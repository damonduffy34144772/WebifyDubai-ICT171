Who We Are

WebifyDubai will provide comprehensive digital solutions tailored towards small businesses lacking a digital platform, including:

  • Website Development: Custom-built websites using Amazon Web Services (AWS) or DigitalOcean for scalability, flexibility and reliability. WebifyDubai will focus on user-friendly and responsive designs for these websites optimized for multiple platforms.
  • Domain Name Assistance: Go through the process of securing domain names on behalf of the client through platforms such as GoDaddy or Namecheap, ensuring professional branding for customers and affordable pricing.
  • Basic CyberSecurity Services: Implementing SSL certification for custom-made websites, setting up firewalls, malware protection, and security audits to protect client's websites and devices.
  • Maintenance & Support: Offering monthly retainers for website updates on customer requests, hosting management, and regular cybersecurity audits to ensure that client's digital assets are secure and functional.
  • E-Commerce Solution: Assist clients in setting up an online store and integrating secure payment gateways.
